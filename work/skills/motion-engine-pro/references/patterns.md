# Pro Motion Patterns

## Composition Pattern Set

1. Hero left / support right
2. Hero right / process left
3. Lower-third emphasis with upper negative space
4. Summary stack with slight horizontal offsets

## Transition Recipes

1. Narrative advance: directional wipe
2. Topic reset: dissolve through background color
3. Emphasis punch: quick zoom in and out
4. Continuity bridge: shared motif persists during transition

## Atmosphere Recipes

1. Vignette: darken edges to drive focus
2. Soft grain: subtle texture to reduce flat digital look
3. Ambient orbs: low-contrast drift for depth without distraction

## Performance Recipes

1. Cache blurred card sprites by parameter tuple
2. Avoid generating random noise in every frame
3. Keep callback math simple and vector-light
