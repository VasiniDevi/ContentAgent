# Styleframe Checklist

## Layout

1. Validate a clear focal element in each frame.
2. Validate one negative-space zone remains untouched.
3. Validate safe margins on left and right edges.
4. Validate slide-to-slide variation in card placement.

## Hierarchy

1. Validate headline contrast and size dominance.
2. Validate support line is visible but subordinate.
3. Validate decorative accents never compete with primary copy.

## Rhythm

1. Validate each slide introduces at least one visual change from the previous slide.
2. Validate transitions match meaning (cut, wipe, dissolve, zoom) instead of repeating one pattern.
3. Validate shared motifs persist across the whole sequence.

## Readability

1. Validate each text block has 12 words or fewer.
2. Validate body text contrast ratio is at least 4.5:1.
3. Validate label text contrast ratio is at least 3.0:1.

## Polish

1. Validate depth through shadows, glow, or atmospheric overlays.
2. Validate no accidental overlaps or clipping.
3. Validate visual noise level stays low and intentional.
